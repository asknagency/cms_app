In a monumental effort to preserve India's rich heritage, the **Archaeological Survey of India (ASI)** has initiated the delicate process of drilling into the historic Konark Sun Temple. The primary objective is to safely remove tons of sand that were packed into the structure's core by British administrators over a century ago to prevent its collapse.

### Modern Engineering Meets Ancient Architecture
Using state-of-the-art non-destructive testing and endoscopic cameras, engineers are carefully mapping the internal voids. This intervention aims to restore structural stability and ensure the UNESCO World Heritage site stands tall for centuries to come.
