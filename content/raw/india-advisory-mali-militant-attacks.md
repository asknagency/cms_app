The Ministry of External Affairs has issued an urgent advisory for Indian nationals residing in **Mali**, urging them to remain strictly indoors. This follows a sudden and violent surge in militant and separatist attacks across the capital region.

### Evacuation Plans on Standby
The Indian Embassy in Bamako is coordinating closely with local authorities. While commercial flights remain suspended, contingency plans for potential evacuation are actively being drafted should the security situation deteriorate further.
