The global energy market faced an unprecedented shock today as Brent crude oil surged past $106 a barrel. **The Strait of Hormuz**, a critical chokepoint for international oil supplies, has seen severe disruptions following recent US-Israeli strikes in the region.

### The Impact on Global Trade
Supply chains are already feeling the pressure. Shipping companies are rerouting vessels around the Cape of Good Hope, adding weeks to delivery times and skyrocketing freight costs. Economists warn that if the blockade continues, global inflation could see another sharp spike heading into Q3 2026.
