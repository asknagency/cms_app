Strengthening Indo-Pacific ties, India and New Zealand formally signed a comprehensive **Free Trade Agreement (FTA)** in New Delhi today. The deal substantially slashes tariffs on agricultural goods, machinery, and digital services.

### A Win-Win for Both Economies
The FTA is projected to double bilateral trade by 2030. Indian IT professionals will see relaxed visa quotas, while New Zealand gains crucial market access for its dairy, timber, and wine sectors.
