India's youth brigade continues to dominate the ranges. On the eighth day of the **Junior Shooting World Cup** in Cairo, the Indian contingent added two more golds, taking their overall tally to 16 medals.

### Next-Gen Sharpshooters
The consistency shown by the Indian squad across the 10m Air Rifle and 25m Pistol events highlights a robust grassroots training infrastructure, setting a highly promising stage for the upcoming 2028 Los Angeles Olympics.
