In a nail-biting final, **Al-Ahli Saudi FC** successfully defended their AFC Champions League Elite crown last night. They edged past first-time finalists, Japan's Machida Zelvia, with a decisive 1-0 victory.

### Saudi Football Supremacy
The solitary goal came in the 78th minute via a spectacular free-kick. This back-to-back championship further cements Saudi Arabia's growing dominance and massive investment in club football across the Asian continent.
