While traditionalists at the main festival maintain strict bans on purely AI-generated cinema for the Palme d'Or, the inaugural **Cannes AI Film Festival** is stealing the spotlight on the Riviera this week. 

### The Future of Storytelling?
Showcasing breathtaking hyper-realistic films rendered entirely by text-to-video models like Google's Veo and OpenAI's Sora, the upstart movement is attracting massive Silicon Valley investment, raising existential questions for Hollywood directors and actors alike.
